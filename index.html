Rochester VegFest is Coming September 15, 2018!

MLK PARK DOWNTOWN ROCHESTER
10AM - 4PM
SUGGESTED DONATION OF $5

Rochester VegFest is brought to you by Animal Rights Rochester and The Rochester Area Vegan society. Let's celebrate our love for animals and compassionate, plant-based food and businesses that are healthy, ethical, and environmentally conscious.

DIRECTIONS

PARKING

Street parking is free on Saturday's! Plus there's a free garage at 194 Court Street, which is about a 5 minute walk from the festival grounds. No quarters necessary.

FAQ

How much does entrance cost?

There is a suggested donation of $5 per adult guest. These donations go directly towards making Rochester's VegFest an unforgetable experience!

What if it rains?

In case of rain: the show must go on! We have a few large tents (plus a larger speaker tent this year.) An umbrella is always a good idea if the forecast calls for rain.

What should I bring?

Please bring a reusable waterbottle as we'll have water stations throughout the festival. There will be space on the grass to spread out and relax, so you may wish to bring a towel, picnic blanket, or yoga mat. There will also be seating in the speaker's tent.
